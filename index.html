4SP
(4simpleproblems)

My primary mission. 4SP is more than just a toolkit; it's a movement towards educational freedom. What started as a simple set of scripts has evolved into a comprehensive suite of utilities used by hundreds of students daily.

The project focuses on quality-of-life improvements, bypassing restrictive network filters, and providing access to essential educational resources that are often unfairly blocked. V5 represents the pinnacle of this journey, featuring a modular architecture and a focus on user privacy.

  • Reached over 500+ users globally
  • Presence in 17+ Schools across 8 US States
  • International reach spanning the UK and Spain

Viro

The ultimate sports hub. Viro was created to fill the gap in real-time, fan-driven sports discussion. It's a platform where passion meets technology, providing a modern space for watch parties and team analysis.

With a focus on speed and community, Viro integrates live score updates with high-performance chat modules. The design language—white, orange, and clean grids—reflects the energy and clarity of the sports world.
