4SP History

Version 1

March 13, 2025

The initial release focused on a Soundboard as the primary feature. This version established the foundation for providing student-centric utilities in a professionally styled package.

Version 2

April 11, 2025

V2 expanded the scope significantly, introducing Games, a Proxy system, and a unique interface featuring jumbled iframes to bypass common restrictions.

Version 3

May 11, 2025

A major design overhaul. V3 introduced the refined aesthetic that would define the project's identity, focusing on a cleaner user experience and improved accessibility.

Version 4

August 23, 2025

The "Massive Update." V4 introduced the sidebar navigation and scaled the library to over 1000+ games, marking the largest expansion in the project's history at that point.

Version 5

December 3, 2025

The jump to V5 brought a completely modern architecture. Despite initial authentication bugs fixed the following day, it reached 52 users within 24 hours.

V5 Release

December 3, 2025

Official launch of the V5 branch, featuring a complete rewrite of the codebase for enhanced performance and security.

DV Launch

January 3, 2026

The DV (Decentralized Version) was released to ensure persistence through local file access, creating an unblockable resource for students across multiple regions.

MAX Edition

January 16th, 2026

The cutting-edge experimental branch of 4SP. Originally introduced with Velium Music, it quickly expanded to reintegrate Vern (the high-performance proxy from V2) and launched brand new modules like Vora for movies and Vana for AI integration—all while sporting a polished, refreshed aesthetic built on the 4SP V5 foundation.
